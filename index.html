Common Questions

What areas do you deliver to?

We deliver within 45 miles of Centuria, WI. This includes Rice Lake, Barron, Cumberland, Spooner, Shell Lake, Turtle Lake, Amery, St. Croix Falls, Siren, Webster, Grantsburg, and all of Polk, Barron, Burnett, and Washburn counties. If you're not sure, give us a call.

Do you offer same-day delivery?

Yes. Call by 10 AM and we'll get you concrete that day, subject to availability. We understand schedules are tight and do our best to accommodate rush orders.

What's the minimum order?

We can work with small pours. Whether you need 2 yards for a sidewalk or 200 yards for a commercial slab, call us to discuss your project — we'll make it work.

Do you deliver on Saturdays?

Yes, Saturday hours are 7 AM to 2 PM. Call ahead to schedule your weekend pour so we can reserve truck availability for you.

What pumping options do you have?

We have boom pump and line pump services. The boom pump can reach over obstacles and into tight spots like basements, second stories, and backyard patios where mixer trucks can't access directly.

Do you spread ag lime?

Yes. We provide agricultural lime spreading to improve soil pH and boost crop yields for farms throughout Northwest Wisconsin. Contact us for pricing and availability.
